Hiraya — Logo Concepts v2

Focused on learning, exam review, and board prep. #5 kept from v1.

Concept 1 — Icon + Wordmark
Exam Pass
Icon + Wordmark · Clean & Academic

A clipboard exam sheet with answer bubbles and a bold checkmark slashed across it — "you passed." Immediately reads as exam/test/review. The sparkle at the check tip adds the Hiraya optimism. Clean, recognizable at every size.

Academic Achievement Clean
Concept 2 — Icon + Wordmark
Bright Review
Icon + Wordmark · Warm & Clever

A lightbulb whose filament is a pencil — the "aha" moment of studying. Learning clicks on, knowledge lights up. The rays around the bulb echo the Golden Hour warmth. Immediately reads as "study + insight" without being generic.

Clever Warm Insightful
Concept 3 — Abstract Symbol
Step Up
Abstract Symbol · Bold & Motivational

Ascending steps from light gold to deep orange — each step is a stage of mastery. A star crowns the top: you made it, you passed the board exam. The gradient progression mirrors the study journey. Reads instantly as "progress" and "leveling up."

Motivational Progress Bold
Concept 4 — Icon + Wordmark
Cap & Book
Icon + Wordmark · Academic & Warm

An open study book with text lines on its pages, crowned by a graduation cap — the full story of Hiraya. You study (book), you pass (cap). The tassel adds a celebratory touch. Academic without being stuffy, warm through the golden palette.

Academic Celebratory Warm
Concept 5 — Wordmark Current Pick
Hiraya Glow
Wordmark · Minimal & Modern

Pure typographic logo — lowercase "hiraya" in Outfit Extra Bold with a gradient wash. The dot of the "i" becomes a tiny glowing sun with rays — the signature detail. Shown on both light and dark backgrounds. Minimal, confident, scales beautifully.

Minimal Modern Typographic
