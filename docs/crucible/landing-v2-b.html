Under the hood

Science-backed.
Not just
another quiz.

Hiraya's adaptive engine combines three proven psychometric and cognitive science methods. Together they build a precise model of what you know — and intelligently close the gaps before exam day.

Item Response Theory SM-2 Spaced Repetition Category Targeting Ability Estimation (θ)
Item Response Theory IRT

Each question carries a difficulty parameter. As you answer, Hiraya estimates your latent ability θ using maximum likelihood estimation — and selects the next item at exactly the right challenge level.

SM-2 Spaced Repetition SM-2

Items you answered incorrectly are re-queued at scientifically optimized intervals. Forgetting curves are flattened. Long-term retention is maximized without over-drilling what you already know.

Category Targeting CAT

Your ability score is tracked independently for all 9 PhilNITS categories. Sessions automatically prioritize the categories where improvement will have the greatest impact on your overall readiness.
