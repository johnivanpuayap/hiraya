Under the hood

An engine that learns as you do.

Most reviewers give you a static question bank. Hiraya gives you a live model of your knowledge — constantly updated, always honest.

Item Response Theory (IRT)
A 3-parameter logistic model computes the probability you answer each question correctly, then selects items at your ability frontier (θ ≈ b). Maximum information, minimum wasted questions.
SM-2 Spaced Repetition
The classic SuperMemo-2 algorithm schedules review intervals based on your response quality. Items you struggle with come back sooner; items you've mastered drift further apart.
Category Targeting
Your weakest categories get the most exposure — proportional to the gap between your current θ and passing threshold. No more studying what you already know.
